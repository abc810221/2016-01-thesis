What is Portfol.io?

Portfol.io is a web-based stock market game where you compete against others in creating the highest returning stock portfolio through buying and selling stocks during trading hours and based on real-time market prices.

You can join a daily or weekly public league, or create a private league of your own! And play in as many leagues as you would like.
